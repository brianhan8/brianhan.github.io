How to Delete Your Data

To delete all your saved products, prices, and excluded keywords from Product Tracker, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Product Tracker app.
  2. Go to Go to the saved item you want to delete.
  3. Tap on Press the trash icon.
  4. Confirm the action when prompted.

All your cloud-synced data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

If you have any issues deleting your data, please contact us at brianhan.github.io@gmail.com.
